Plutus customers with an Euro based Plutus accout can buy their own countries gift cards as well as all other Euro based gift cards. UK customers can only buy UK gift cards.

A 10€/£ giftcard costs 1 Plu plus a variable fee. These are the current fees the community could find out:

Amount of giftcards redeemed: - Fees:
1-3 -------- 30%
4-9 -------- 40%
10-20 ----- 50%
21-49 ----- 60%
50-74 ----- 70%
75+ -------- 80%
So for the 21st giftcard, you pay 1 Plu and 6€/6£. It is assumed that these fees reset back to 30% monthly.
